
Data From:
http://www.stsci.edu/science/starburst/Kurucz.html

Nomenclature: txgymz.dat, where
x is the temperature from 4000 to 60000 (as below)
y is 10 x log(g) (available as below); and
z is one of the 4 available metallicities (p03, p00, m06, m10)


Reference: Kurucz, R. L. 1992, in IAU Symposium 149,
The Stellar Populations of Galaxies, ed. B. Barbuy & A. Renzini (Dordrecht:Kluwer), 225 

Units are Angstroms for wavelengths and erg/s/cm/cm/A for fluxes.
Listed are Astrophysical Fluxes (with a factor of pi).
